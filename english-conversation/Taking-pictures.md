# Taking pictures

[video 1:07:05](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OHK-xsvW0TQ)

## English

---

```
William: Excuse me, will you take a picture of us?

Susan: Sure. This is really nice camera.

William: Thanks, my parents gave it to me.

Susan: How do you use it?

William: You press this button here.

Susan: Come a little closer. Wait, that's too close. Move little to the left. Move back a little farther. Ok, stay right there.

William: Do you mean here?

Susan: Yes, that's good. Oh, I can't get it to work.

William: You need to hold down the button for 3 seconds.

Susan: Ok, I got it. Are you ready?

William: Yes.

Susan: Smile.
```
